About Me

Zachary Michael Goldman is a student of Computer Science and Political Science at Tufts University from Baltimore, MD. He went to the Park School of Baltimore for 14 years, before starting college at the University of Richmond in VA. He transferred following his freshman year, and became involved with Greek Life. He enjoys making music, playing video games, working out, and reading in his spare time. He has a brother, a mother, and a father, all of whom live in Baltimore.
